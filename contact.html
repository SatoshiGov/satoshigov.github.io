Frequently Asked Questions

Member states of the United Nations, or "macronations", as micronationalists call them, do not recognize the Democratic Republic of Satoshi. We are working tirelessly to gain recognition from these nations. We do however, have formal foreign relations and recognition with other established micronations.

It is not possible to live in the Democratic Republic of Satoshi. It is however possible to visit our territorial claims, by sending an email to contact@satoshigov.org and requesting a visa. This must be done 10 business days before your visit. The Ministry of Internal Affairs may freely deny visas.

Becoming a citizen is quite easy. Simply head over to our citizenship application page and fill out the form. The Ministry of Internal Affairs may freely reject your application.
