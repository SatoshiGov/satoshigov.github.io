Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation

The Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation (MSTI), directly oversees the Satoshi Space and Air Force, a governmental organisation with the primary goal of scientific research in outerspace and aeronautics.

Departments

The Ministry directly oversees the Satoshi Space and Air Force, the official space agency of Satoshi and the regulatory aviation agency. More information on the aforementioned organisation can be read here.
